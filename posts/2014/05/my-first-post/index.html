Hello World

Date Wed 07 May 2014 Tags python / pelican / blog

For a while now I've been toying with the idea to start a blog to serve as a place to post brain dumps, rants, solutions and generally interesting technology and IT content.

Today (or rather, yesterday) however a colleague made a bet with me that I wouldn't have a blog within 24 hours. So, to prove him wrong and win lunch in the process, I've this evening gone and set up this Pelican based blog. Fortunately I've looked at some of the questions on how to do this before and had previously concluded that Pelican would be as good a choice as any, certainly to start out with, and likely possibly permanently.

So, sorry Matt, but you lose. ;)
